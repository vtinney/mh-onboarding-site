# Onboarding guide: air pollution, health impact assessment, and our projects

---

## How to use this guide

Welcome aboard. This guide is here to help you get up to speed in your first few months. You do not need to read it cover to cover on day one. Here is a suggested path:

1. Use Part 0, practical onboarding, as your week-one setup checklist (accounts, training, logistics). 
2. Start with Part 1, the primer. It explains what air pollution is, how it harms health, and how greenhouse gases fit into the picture. 
3. Move to Part 2, on health impact assessment. 
4. Read Part 3, on the projects. The Monterrey project is where most of your time will go, so that section is the most detailed, and it includes a team map and a project timeline.
5. Skim Part 4, the people, and come back to it as you start meeting everyone.
6. Read Part 5 (a suggested first few weeks) and Part 6 (how we will work together) early.

A note on the reading list. The list is not meant to be exhaustive or to be read all at once. Treat it as a list to return to.
